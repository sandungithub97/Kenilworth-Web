We Are Thrilled to share the remarkable success of our Drone Pilot Passing Out Ceremony, Amidst the picturesque setting of General Sir John Kotelawala Defense University's Southern Campus, history was made on April 7th as Kenilworth Drones proudly hosted the momentous Drone Pilot Passing Out Ceremony. The event witnessed the convergence of esteemed guests, industry leaders, and visionaries, marking a watershed moment in Sri Lanka's agricultural landscape.

Skilled drone pilots
With unbridled joy and a sense of accomplishment, Kenilworth Drones announces a groundbreaking achievement: the successful flight of 100 drones and the graduation of an elite squadron comprising 100 skilled pilots. This milestone not only cements Kenilworth Drones' position as a trailblazer in agricultural drone services but also signifies a giant leap forward for Sri Lanka's farming sector.
