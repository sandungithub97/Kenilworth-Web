Kenilworth International, a pioneering force in the field of agriculture drone technology, stands as the largest drone service provider in Sri Lanka. With a fleet of over 100 drones and a team of skilled pilots, the company is at the forefront of transforming the agricultural landscape of the country. This year, we proudly hosted a delegation from DJI, the world's leading drone manufacturer, who visited our facilities on April 29th, 2024.

Discussing Future Business Plans with DJI Technology
The DJI delegation's visit was a milestone event for Kenilworth International, highlighting our ongoing commitment to innovation and excellence. During their visit, the delegates had the opportunity to engage with our Managing Director, Kavinda Dissanayaka, a visionary entrepreneur known for his passion for niche markets and his belief in creating the future. The discussions centered around enhancing our operational efficiency and exploring new avenues for collaboration in the drone technology sector.

Pioneering Agriculture Drone Technology


At Kenilworth International, we believe in creating the future by investing in cutting-edge technology and fostering strong partnerships with global leaders like DJI.
As we continue to expand our operations and explore new possibilities, our commitment to innovation, excellence, and sustainability remains unwavering. We are proud to lead the charge in revolutionizing agriculture in Sri Lanka and are excited about the opportunities that lie ahead.

As the pioneers of agriculture drone technology in Sri Lanka, Kenilworth International is dedicated to providing innovative solutions that enhance productivity and sustainability in the agriculture sector. Our drones are used for various applications, including crop monitoring, precision spraying, and data collection, enabling farmers to make informed decisions that optimize their yields and reduce environmental impact.
In conclusion, Kenilworth International's partnership with DJI and our position as the largest drone service provider in Sri Lanka underscore our dedication to advancing the agriculture sector through technology. With a visionary leader like Kavinda Dissanayaka at the helm, our company is poised to set new benchmarks in the industry and pave the way for a brighter, more sustainable future for agriculture in Sri Lanka.
