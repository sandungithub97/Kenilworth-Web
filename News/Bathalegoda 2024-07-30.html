Insights from Industry Experts Lessons
The session was led by the Managing Director of Kenilworth International, an entrepreneur with a passion for investing in niche markets. Known for his belief in creating the future, he shared insights into how state-of-the-art drone technology can revolutionize traditional agricultural practices.

Empowering Future Agricultural Leaders


The event aimed to equip the next generation of agricultural professionals with cutting-edge knowledge and skills. By demonstrating the application of drones in modern agriculture, Kenilworth International highlighted the pivotal role technology plays in enhancing productivity and sustainability in the agricultural sector.
The lecture provided an in-depth overview of the agriculture drone platform in Sri Lanka. Students were introduced to advanced drone models such as the DJI Agras T20P RTK, and learned about optimizing flight planning for various agricultural tasks. The practical session allowed students to engage directly with the technology, offering hands-on experience that underscored theoretical concepts.
