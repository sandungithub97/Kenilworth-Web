Security Shields to Agri-tech Pioneers:
THE KENILWORTH JOURNEY.

Founded in 2017, Kenilworth began its mission as a trusted IT solutions provider, safeguarding businesses with cutting-edge security measures. Early on, we collaborated with industry leaders in the global ICT security space, honing our expertise in protecting critical data.

Fueled by innovation, we soon ventured into the burgeoning field of Agri-Technology, aligning ourselves with our sister company's agricultural endeavors. Here, we proudly became the technology partner for the nation's first digital platform dedicated to the entire agriculture value chain.

But our story doesn't end there. Driven by a passion for progress, Kenilworth soared to new heights, establishing ourselves as the largest owner of agricultural drone fleets and trained drone pilots in the country. With this accomplishment, we're not just setting industry standards, we're revolutionizing the way agriculture operates. But our innovation doesn't stop there. We've also pioneered the "Drone Operation Centre" solution, a first-of-its-kind command center optimizing drone operations for maximum efficiency and impact.

Today, Kenilworth stands as a testament to continuous evolution and unwavering commitment. We bridge the gap between IT prowess and agricultural innovation, empowering progress and shaping the future of farming.
